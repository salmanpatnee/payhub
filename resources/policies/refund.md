# Refund Policy

## 1. General Policy

All payments made for our consultancy, guidance, tutorial, and advisory services are subject to this Refund Policy. By purchasing our services, you acknowledge and agree to the terms outlined below.

## 2. Nature of Services

Due to the customized and time-based nature of our services, refunds are limited. Once a service has been initiated, resources, time, and expertise are allocated, making most transactions non-refundable.

## 3. Eligibility for Refunds

Refunds will only be considered under the following circumstances:

**Eligible Cases:**

- The service was not delivered at all;
- A duplicate payment was made due to a technical error.

## 4. Non-Refundable Situations

Refunds will not be granted in the following cases:

- Change of mind after confirmation;
- Misunderstanding of the nature of services;
- Delays caused by incomplete, inaccurate, or late client input;
- Dissatisfaction based on subjective expectations;
- Services that have already been delivered, partially or fully;
- Failure to use or apply the provided guidance or materials.

## 5. Service Commencement

Once work has commenced, the client acknowledges that:

- The service is considered in progress;
- Payments become non-refundable;
- Any cancellation request may not be honored.

## 6. Revisions in Place of Refunds

Where applicable, we may offer revisions or reasonable adjustments instead of a refund, provided the request aligns with the original agreed scope of service.

## 7. Refund Process

If a client believes they are eligible for a refund:

- A request must be submitted through official communication channels;
- Supporting details must be provided;
- Each request will be reviewed on a case-by-case basis.

We reserve the right to approve or reject any refund request at our sole discretion, based on the criteria outlined in this policy.

## 8. Processing Time

Approved refunds, if any, will be processed within a reasonable timeframe using the original payment method, subject to payment provider processing times.

## 9. Abuse of Policy

Any attempt to misuse or exploit this Refund Policy, including fraudulent claims or bad-faith disputes, may result in:

- Denial of refund requests;
- Suspension or termination of services;
- Further action as deemed necessary.

## 10. Policy Updates

We reserve the right to modify this Refund Policy at any time. Continued use of our services constitutes acceptance of any updated terms.
