## STRIDE Threat Register

| Threat ID | Category | Component | Disposition | Mitigation Plan |
|-----------|----------|-----------|-------------|-----------------|
| T-02-01-01 | Elevation of Privilege | Registration endpoint | mitigate | Remove `Features::registration()` from config/fortify.php — Fortify deregisters GET /register and POST /register entirely; no route exists to exploit |
| T-02-01-02 | Information Disclosure | HandleInertiaRequests auth.user serialization | mitigate | Replace raw `$request->user()` (full Eloquent model) with explicit array: only id, name, email, roles. Fields like two_factor_secret, remember_token are never serialized to page props |
| T-02-01-03 | Elevation of Privilege | spatie role middleware not registered | mitigate | Register aliases in bootstrap/app.php BEFORE route group definitions; without registration, role:admin middleware silently fails with RuntimeException |
| T-02-01-04 | Tampering | emailVerification bypass in tests | mitigate | Remove `Features::emailVerification()` from fortify.php — eliminates the /email/verify redirect that could mask test failures (Pitfall 6) |
</threat_model>
